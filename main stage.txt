Converation System
------------------------
The player can ask other characters about topics and characters of interest. The player can ask one character about another character to learn about their traits and preferences.

Traits and Preferences
---------------------------
Characters have a set of traits and preferences. Traits are attributes of a character (strong, fearless, etc). Preferences are the likes and dislikes of a character. Traits and preferences are discovered through activities that use them or by finding out from other people. 

Micro Quests
----------------
Through directed conversations and activities the player can take part in a number of small quests to learn more about the clan, it's members and the world.
